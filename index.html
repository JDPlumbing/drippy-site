What is Drippy?

Drippy is a logic-driven tool designed by JD Plumbing to bring speed, clarity, and professionalism to every plumbing interaction. From diagnostics to estimates, it's your new best friend on the job.

How it Works

1. Diagnose

Describe the issue or upload a checklist. Drippy walks you through it, step-by-step.

2. Estimate

Drippy uses permit logic, pricing tools, and your inputs to build an accurate job scope.

3. Book or Dispatch

Generate printouts, book the job, or share it with the field team instantly.
