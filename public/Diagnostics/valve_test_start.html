Self Diagnostics Help

Before you begin, it's important to follow safety best practices to avoid injury or damage to your property. This simple DIY Valve Test helps you check if your emergency shutoff valves are working correctly.

⚠️ DO NOT skip or modify any steps.
⚠️ SERIOUS damage can occur if instructions are ignored.

No tools are required.
In fact, we strongly recommend against using tools for this test. All valves should turn easily by hand only—about the same pressure you'd use to open a soda bottle.

DO NOT FORCE any valve.
Pros don’t, and neither should you.

It's smart to keep a towel, small container, or wet/dry vac nearby just in case there's a drip.

If you're ever unsure or uncomfortable, you can book a Diagnostic Visit and let a technician perform this test safely.
